Pic Patrol
<www.picpatrol.com>
Jack Szwergold's personal photo website featuring street photography from in and around the New York City area. The site is dynamically generated by a customized CMS (content management system) which was hand coded from the ground up using PHP, MySQL and Javascript. Photos from the site have been featured on NPR's Studio 360, & Italy's Garage Magazine.
